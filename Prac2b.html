Maggi Making Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 teaspoon of oil
  • 1 packet of Maggi tastemaker (included with noodles)
  • Optional vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 cups of water in a pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of oil to the boiling water.
  3. Break the Maggi noodles into 4 parts and add them to the boiling water.
  4. Cook the noodles for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the Maggi tastemaker and mix well.
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes until the noodles are fully cooked and the water is mostly absorbed.
  7. If adding vegetables, add them along with the noodles and cook until tender.
  8. Turn off the heat and let the noodles sit for a minute before serving.

Serving Suggestion

  • Serve hot with a sprinkle of chopped fresh coriander.
  • Enjoy with a side of your favorite beverage.
